Why “NotTooLate”?

From your 30s onward, muscle strength, balance, and bone density decline quietly — increasing injury and loss of independence.

Walking supports heart health, but it does not preserve strength. Structured resistance training does.

The good news: it is never too late to slow, stop, or reverse this decline.
